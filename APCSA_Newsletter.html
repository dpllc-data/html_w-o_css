A Poem from Author Unknown

The 50%

Many have come and many have gone;
Many realize far too late what they did wrong.

They sign up for their course
and they say I'll complete it on my weeks off.
They put it off, off, off,
and when their instructor calls they may even scoff.

Finally one day they said that I will start this course.
I'll finish it in just a few weeks using my force.

After 2 grueling weeks they had just passed the halfway point of module two.
It is only then do they begin to think when is semester one due?

Perhaps I should look at my pacing guide again?
I should never have waited this long to begin.

This course needs attention almost every day;
It's time to catch up… no more time for delay.
- A poem

DBAs

1. A DBA is the easiest and fastest 25 points that you will ever earn in THIS course. You can prepare for your DBA by reviewing your notes (programming concepts) and the programs that you wrote in the prior sections.
2. DBAs are a cornerstone of Florida virtual school.

Tips for Course Success

1. Read and complete all activities.
2. Complete all EIMACS labs. We write about 75% of the quiz and exam questions from EIMACS labs.
3. Take notes. Reach out to Mr. Shelton if you need help putting together a notetaking system.
4. Check out the Resources section on the course homepage.
5. Use the course email system to reach out for help!

Stay on Pace

1. Print out your pacing guide and place it in a visible area.
2. Turn in assignments as you complete them. This will allow you to see if you are doing something wrong and will cut down on your resubmissions.
